Industry AI Case Study

AI-Powered Milk Tray Inspection System

A real AI system built for a real factory — transforming quality control with computer vision and automation.

Client: KMF Nandhini Milk Factory, Karnataka
Built by: NeuraGlobal
Type: Industry-grade AI Proof of Concept

NeuraGlobal successfully designed and delivered a full AI-powered inspection PoC for KMF Nandhini Milk Factory. The solution demonstrated how computer vision + automation can transform traditional dairy operations, earning strong appreciation from factory leadership.

This project marks NeuraGlobal's first industry-level AI deployment.

The Industry Problem

Manual & Repetitive

Traditional inspection relies on human workers performing the same checks endlessly.

Prone to Human Error

Fatigue and inconsistency lead to quality issues slipping through.

Difficult to Scale

High throughput demands make manual inspection a bottleneck.

Inconsistent Across Shifts

Quality standards vary between different workers and time periods.

Quality control needed a fast, reliable, and automated approach that works directly on the factory floor.

The NeuraGlobal Solution

A real-time AI + Computer Vision system integrated with automation hardware

Custom Computer Vision Model

Trained specifically for milk tray inspection with high accuracy.

OpenCV Image Pipeline

Robust visual detection and preprocessing for consistent results.

Live Camera Feed

Real-time monitoring with instant visual feedback.

Robotic Arm Integration

Automatic sorting based on AI classification decisions.

Perfect/Not-Perfect Classification

Binary decision logic for clear, actionable outcomes.

Image Testing Module

Upload and validate images for accuracy improvement.

This wasn't a demo—it was a deployable industrial PoC.
